Farm products

Freshness you can taste

Forget blueberries that travel for miles. Ours are picked at their peak, meaning you get a taste explosion of summer with every mouthful. Plus, all that freshness means they're packed with nutrients - a win for your taste buds and your body.

When you choose Castleton Farm blueberries, you're choosing a local farm. This keeps money flowing in the community and reduces those food miles. Fewer miles mean a smaller carbon footprint, which is good for you, good for the planet.
