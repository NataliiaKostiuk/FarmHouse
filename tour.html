Natural Products - The Key to Good Health

We care about your taste and well-being, which is why we grow eco-friendly berries, vegetables, and greens without chemicals or pesticides. What awaits you on our farm?

🍓 Sweet strawberries and juicy blueberries - taste them fresh from the field!

🍅 Greenhouses filled with fragrant tomatoes, fresh cabbage, and crispy carrots.

🥛 Fresh milk from our cows, rich in calcium and vitamins.

🥚 Organic eggs from free-range chickens, raised in a natural and healthy environment.

You can observe the farming process, taste fresh produce straight from the garden, and even take part in the harvest!

Exciting Activities for the Whole Family

🚜 Love adventure? Try our quad bike rentals! Ride through scenic farm trails, enjoy the fresh air, and get your adrenaline pumping.

👧 For our little guests, we have a children's playground with swings, slides, and a sandbox! While kids have fun, parents can relax and enjoy the peaceful countryside.

Farm Café - Delicious and Healthy

After the tour, stop by our cozy café, where we serve fresh, farm-to-table meals made from organic ingredients.

🥤 Milkshakes made from fresh farm milk

🍞 Homemade pastries

🥗 Fresh vegetable salads

🍰 Berry desserts
